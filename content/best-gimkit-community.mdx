If you're into Gimkit Creative, you've probably noticed there isn't a great central place to share maps, get feedback, or connect with other builders that's open to everyone. That's exactly what [GlimWorld](https://padlet.com/GlimWorldForever/glimworld-romw9j63mcbzwbjb) is trying to fix.

## What Is GlimWorld?

GlimWorld is a community built specifically for Gimkit Creative players and map builders. It's a place to share your creations, discover maps made by others, and connect with people who are just as into Gimkit Creative as you are. Or, you can just come hang out and chat with other builders!

## Why Join?

- **Share your maps** and get real feedback from other builders
- **Discover** creative maps you won't find anywhere else
- **Connect** with a community that actually cares

## How to Join

Head over to the [GlimWorld community Padlet](https://padlet.com/GlimWorldForever/glimworld-romw9j63mcbzwbjb) and introduce yourself. It's free and open to everyone.
